COVID-19 HEALTH AND SAFETY PROTOCOLS:

• All attendees will be temperature screened before entry. Patrons with elevated temperatures will not be allowed to enter.
• All attendees must wear a mask for enterance and any time they are away from their seats or designated areas (masks will be provided to those without).
• No outside food or drinks.
• Touch-free donation options.
• Restroom and elevator occupancy are limited and monitored at all times.
• Frequent cleaning on touch surfaced and use of electrostatic disenfecant devices.
• Multiple hand sanitizer locations are available throughout the venue.
• Seating in the LHUMC Sanctuary will be every other row with two seats empty between parties for adequate social distancing.
• Olmos Ensemble staff and volunteers will be temperature screened before entering the sanctuary and will not be allowed to enter if elevated.
