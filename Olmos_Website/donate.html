For the last quarter century, Olmos Ensemble has relied exclusively on the generous support of donors to present world-class chamber music to San Antonio audiences.

We have created an ARTIST FUND - a way for you to support our musicians through donations that will be designated specifically to pay musicians for cancelled events and ensure our music continues to be brought to our audiences through radio and social media broadcasts. Please consider a unique donation to our ARTIST FUND today. You can use the PayPal icon above to donate online, or mail a check to the address below.

Whether you donate at a performance, by mail, or on-line through our secure PayPal account, your gift helps to support an invaluable cultural resource for South Texas. Olmos Ensemble is a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit organization and your gift is tax deductible to the extent allowable by law.

You can donate in person at Olmos Ensemble concerts, by clicking the donate button on this page or by mail.

To order a CD, please be sure to let us which CD you are ordering, along with your mailing address and email address, so we can be sure to get it to you!

We are able to accept donations of stock or securities as well as bequest gifts through your will or living trust. You can also name Olmos Ensemble as a beneficiary on your retirement plan, life insurance policy or your donor-advised fund. By filling out a form that is entirely separate from your will, you can review and adjust beneficiary designations at any time.
